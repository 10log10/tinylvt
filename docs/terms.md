## Service Description

TinyLVT provides a platform for fair allocation of shared spaces through auction-based mechanisms. Communities use TinyLVT to run auctions that determine who gets which space and at what price.

## Accounts

You must provide a valid email address when creating an account. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your account credentials. You may delete your account at any time from your profile settings.

When you delete your account, your personal information is removed. However, financial history you produced through community currency transactions or auction results is preserved with your identity anonymized, since other members depend on this history for their records. Deleting the community itself fully deletes all associated records.

## Subscriptions and Billing

TinyLVT offers a free tier and a paid tier. The free tier includes 50 MB of storage per community. The paid tier provides 2 GB of storage and is billed monthly ($5/month) or annually ($50/year) per community.

Payments are processed by Stripe. By subscribing, you agree to Stripe's [terms of service](https://stripe.com/legal/consumer).

## Cancellation Policy

You may cancel your subscription at any time from the Billing page in your community settings. When you cancel:

- Your subscription remains active until the end of the current billing period.
- After the billing period ends, your community reverts to the free tier.
- No data is deleted. If your storage exceeds the free tier limit, you will not be able to upload new content until you reduce usage or resubscribe.

## Refund Policy
